My Story

My background is in marketing and consulting but my passion is creative thinking and design after having the opportunity to assist with a company's website only to expand on my curiosity years later.

I studied Marketing at California State Northridge and pursued a career in San Diego, CA as a Marketing Coordinator where I wore many hats including photographer, blogger, literature design and event planning. I assisted in planning and executing the redesign of the company's website where I strived in the creative aspect and became interested in the web development as well as the design.

Having moved several times since my marketing coordinator days and developing different skills in other areas I pursued UX Design to fuel my creativity once again and now back in my hometown of Bakersfield, CA.

Now I spend my time exploring different areas of the UX, reading, favorite genre being murder mystery and love to dance.
