ActiveXplorer

An app that allows you to explore and stay physically active

Problem Statement

Players of the scavenger hunt need a way to simply set up and play a game that challenges them and keeps them active because they need to work, individually or in a group, to successfully fulfill the goals of the game. We know this to be true when players are interacting and finishing games provided within the app.

Proposed Solution

An app that allows players to be physically active while enjoying an easy to use, visually appealing scavenger hunt app. The user must be able to discover their surroundings, whether individually or in a group, and have the satisfaction of completing activities.

Card Sorting

Closed Card-Sorting allowed me to discover how others would categorize and organize topics based on a given category. Based on the results, we were able to determine insight as to how the user would perceive the app and want to navigate it. Therefore, My Hunts were actually placed on the home screen allowing users to quickly navigate to their hunts and sharing to social media seemed more relevant at the end of a hunt where the user could share their hunt on social media.
